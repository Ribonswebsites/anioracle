Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information AniOracle collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

AniOracle does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We store the following locally on your device only: (a) Your Gemini API key (if provided) stored in your browser's localStorage; (b) Your cookie consent preference; (c) Your theme preference and trivia score.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit: personal identification information, browsing history, IP addresses, location data, or any data to our own servers. AniOracle has no backend server or database.

Third-Party Services

AniOracle uses the following third-party services: (a) Jikan API — public MyAnimeList data API, no personal data sent; (b) Google Gemini API — if you provide your own API key, your queries are sent to Google. Please review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com; (c) GitHub Pages — for hosting this site. GitHub may collect standard server logs.

Cookies & Local Storage

AniOracle uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember your preferences locally on your device. No data is sent to any server. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser data.

Children's Privacy

AniOracle is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us.

Data Security

Since AniOracle stores data only on your local device and has no server-side database, your data remains on your device at all times. Your Gemini API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete or provide. You can remove all locally stored data by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the latest update at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Contact page within the app.
